An easy chicken tortellini soup recipe!

Description

Cook this super easy chicken tortellini soup in less than 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 (300g) pack Coles Chicken Tortellini
  • 1 (450g) bag Coles Kitchen Chicken Style Soup Mix Kit

Steps

  1. In a medium sized saucepan add 1.25L (5 cups) of boiled water.
  2. Empty contents of bag and stock powder sachet into the saucepan. Bring to the boil.
  3. Once boiling, turn down to a gentle simmer and cook for 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  4. While the soup is simmering, cook the chicken tortellini in a large pan of simmering salted water for 3 - 4 minutes until piping hot and the pasta is tender. Drain and add to a soup bowl.
  5. Once the soup is cooked, pour over the tortellini and serve.
